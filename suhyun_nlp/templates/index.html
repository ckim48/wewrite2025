About WeWrite

WeWrite is a platform where anyone can unleash their imagination and freely express their thoughts and emotions through writing. Users can choose their preferred genre, and each story is divided into five parts: exposition, rising action, climax, falling action, and resolution. When one user writes the exposition, another user continues with the next section, creating a collaborative writing experience.

For users with physical disabilities, all stories are converted into audiobooks and are available in both English and Korean. This feature helps both writers and readers, regardless of any limitations they may have, to transcend the boundaries of creativity and experience a sense of connection with others.

Highlights

Select

You don’t need to read the entire story from the beginning - simply browse a concise summary of the previous parts, then choose the story and section you’d like to contribute to.

Collaborate

Unleash your imagination and continue the story in a way that flows naturally from the previous part, working together with others to create ‘our’ story.

Immerse

Whether the story is in English or Korean, and no matter the reader’s abilities, everyone can enjoy new stories through both sight and sound.
